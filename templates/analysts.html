Personality Prediction System

Analysts Personality Type

Analysts have the traits Intuition and Thinking in common and tend to be more rational people. They use their curiosity to come up with creative solutions and innovative strategies. Individuals who fall under the category Analyst tend to be relentless self-improvers and independent minds. The four analyst personalities are:

1. Architects (INTJ) are among the rarest personality types, they have to analyse everything and bleed perfectionism. These individuals are imaginative but decisive, display curiosity, ambition and rarely struggle with keeping their focus.

2. Logicians (INTP) are proud of their creativity, unique outlook and intellect. They’re enthusiastic problem solvers.

3. Commanders (ENTJ) are natural leaders who embody confidence and charisma. They love a good challenge and believe that they can achieve any goal.

4. Debaters (ENTP) are on a constant quest for knowledge and have most fun when they can argue on a topic of choice, whether it aligns with their personal opinion doesn’t matter. All debaters want is to expand their horizons and show off their quick wit. It’s their understanding of a fun time.
